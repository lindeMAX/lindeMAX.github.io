Muon Observation Network

I am currently working with a few high school students on a muon observation network at the SFN.

The long term goal is to do cosmic weather forcasts or predict cosmic bursts. This could come in handy for particle phsicists, because it is nearly impossable to shield muons.

Check out the projects website for more information.
